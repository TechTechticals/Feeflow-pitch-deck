How long does setup take?
We get you live in 5 days with dedicated onboarding support — account setup, data import, and staff training included.

Yes. Our team helps you migrate all student records, fee history, and batches from Excel into FeeFlow during onboarding.
Can I manage multiple branches?
Absolutely. FeeFlow supports unlimited branches under one account with separate data and consolidated reports across all locations.
Is my student data secure?
Yes. Data is encrypted at rest and in transit, stored with bank-level security, and backed up automatically in real time.
Can parents receive automated reminders?
Yes. FeeFlow sends automatic WhatsApp and SMS reminders to parents before and after fee due dates — no manual follow-up needed.
